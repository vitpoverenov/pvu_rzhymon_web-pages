Immunity

Cordyceps, with its rich history in traditional medicine and growing body of scientific research, has emerged as a promising natural solution for enhancing immunity. Its unique blend of bioactive compounds provides valuable support to the immune system, helping the body ward off infections and diseases

Boosts immune cell activity

Cordyceps has been shown to increase the activity of certain immune cells, such as natural killer cells and T cells, which play a key role in fighting off infections and cancer cells.

Regulates immune response

Cordyceps may help to balance the immune system by regulating the production of cytokines, which are signaling molecules that control immune cell activity. This may help to prevent excessive inflammation or an overactive immune response.

Enhanced antibody production

A review published in Phytotherapy Research (2019) highlighted the potential of Cordyceps as a functional food or supplement for immune support, based on its capacity to regulate both innate and adaptive immunity.

Antiviral activity

Cordyceps has been shown to increase the production of immunoglobulin, which are antibodies that help to recognize and fight off pathogens.

Anti-inflammatory properties
