Internal organic Tonic

Recent research has shed light on its organ tonic potential, which refers to its ability to improve the function of various organs in the body.

Cardiovascular health

It can improve heart function by reducing oxidative stress, inflammation, and cholesterol levels. It may also help regulate blood pressure and improve blood flow

Blood sugar regulation

Cordyceps may help regulate blood sugar levels by improving insulin sensitivity and glucose metabolism, making it potentially beneficial for individuals with type 2 diabetes.

Respiratory health

It can improve lung function, reduce inflammation, and dilate airways, making it potentially beneficial for individuals with respiratory conditions such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), and bronchitis.

Liver protection

Cordyceps has been shown to increase the production of immunoglobulin, which are antibodies that help to recognize and fight off pathogens.

Kidney health

Cordyceps has been traditionally used in chinese medicine for protective effects on renal tissue and for conditions such as chronic nephritis or pyelonephritis or general renal dysfunction. It may also improve renal function in patients with chronic kidney disease and help prevent kidney transplant rejection.
